Teammate Shooting Impact

Players must have 150+ teammate FGA with the player on the court. Each qualifying teammate needs 25+ teammate FGA with the player, 25+ teammate FGA without the player, and 10+ shared minutes.

Stat Explained

Adjusted WOWY compares teammate shooting with the player on the court versus that teammate's own baseline without the player.

Formula

Σ((teammate result with player - teammate result without player) × weight) / Σ(weight)
